This infographic, titled "5 Tips for Digital Literacy Awareness," provides a foundational guide for navigating the digital world effectively and safely. It defines Digital Literacy as the ability to find, evaluate, create, and communicate information using digital technologies.
The infographic highlights five essential areas of focus: 

Safe Internet Practices: This section emphasizes security by recommending the use of strong passwords, enabling two-factor authentication, and avoiding the sharing of personal information online.

Essential Digital Tools: It suggests using productivity apps, cloud storage, and collaboration platforms to improve both learning and work efficiency.

Verify Information: To combat misinformation and fake news, this tip advises users to check multiple sources and look for credible authors.

Build Digital Skills: This encourages continuous learning through online courses, practicing with new software, and staying updated on emerging technological trends.

Visual Representation: The infographic uses various illustrations of people interacting with digital devices—such as laptops, tablets, and magnifying glasses—to represent searching, evaluating, and collaborating in a digital environment.
